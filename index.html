Clinical Intelligence Platform (CLIP)

Role: Senior UX Designer

Domain: Healthcare AI

Problem

Clinical decision-making relies on fragmented and complex datasets, making interpretation slow and cognitively demanding.

  • Cognitive overload due to dense data
  • Disconnected workflows
  • High dependency on technical expertise

Goal

Simplify clinical data into actionable insights using AI-driven workflows.

Approach

Collaborated with clinicians and data teams to map workflows and identify friction points across decision-making journeys.

Solution

Designed an AI-powered platform that transforms raw data into intuitive, structured insights.

Impact

  • Reduced decision-making time
  • Improved usability across teams
  • Enabled scalable AI workflows

Key Design Decisions

  • Shift from raw data → insights
  • Progressive disclosure for complexity
  • AI as assistant, not replacement

Learnings

  • Simplification drives adoption
  • AI UX must build trust
  • Complex systems need strong structure
